When a toggle is flipped, immediately call PATCH /api/notifications/preferences with the updated emailEnabled value for that type — no save button needed. **Loading state** — While GET /api/notifications/preferences is resolving, show 9 shimmer placeholder rows in place of the toggles.

**On mount**, call GET /api/notifications/preferences to load the current emailEnabled state for all 9 types, the alertDeliveryEmail, and lastTestEmailSentAt. Wire all 9 toggle states and the delivery email field to this response.
